Title: Searching

Year: 2023

Material: 10x15 Inkjet print

Description: The poster aims to visualize the text from a film poster into a different genre than it was originally in. Through visual deconstruction of each genre, the poster reconstructs the text “Searching” and “You are what you hide”, from the mystery/thriller movie ‘Searching’, into the genre of monster horror.
