Kyujin Han is a designer born in South Korea and currently based in Chicago. His work focuses on creating a visual experience that is thorough and comfortable in graphic design and photography. Living in a visually-overwhelming time his work speaks in a quiet, rhythmic, yet structural and logical way that exists in the digital world. This ethos further extends to his research, where materials and physical connections communicate clarity and delight to the audience.
